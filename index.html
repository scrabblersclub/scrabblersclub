ABOUT

The Scrabbler Club aims to foster a community of civil engineering students and professionals, promoting technical knowledge, innovation, and collaboration through word games, quizzes, and industry-related activities.
Objectives:
1. Enhance vocabulary and knowledge related to civil engineering.
2. Encourage teamwork and problem-solving skills.
3. Provide a platform for networking with industry professionals.
4. Promote innovation and creativity in civil engineering.Open to civil engineering students and professionals.
*Events organised *
1. Scrabble Tournaments: Civil engineering-themed word games.
2. Quiz Nights: Technical quizzes on civil engineering topics.
3. Guest Lectures: Industry experts sharing experiences and insights.
4. Case Study Presentations: Analysis and discussion of real-world projects.
5. Site Visits: Tours of construction sites and infrastructure projects.
6. Design Competitions: Innovative solutions to civil engineering challenges.
Benefits:
1. Enhance technical knowledge and vocabulary.
2. Develop teamwork and problem-solving skills.
3. Network with industry professionals.
4. Improve communication and presentation skills.
5. Stay updated on industry trends and innovations.
